Our Solution: Secure, Seamless, Swift

Atleos Connect introduces a mobile-first workflow that eliminates physical cards and contact. The user's mobile device becomes the key, transforming the ATM into a simple, secure cash dispensing touchpoint.

1

Initiate in App

User securely selects withdrawal amount in their trusted mobile banking app, anytime, anywhere.

2

Get Secure OTP

The app generates a dynamic, time-sensitive One-Time Password (OTP) for the specific transaction.

3

Enter at ATM

User approaches any enabled ATM and enters the OTP. Proximity verification ensures the user is present.

4

Collect Cash

The ATM securely dispenses the pre-staged cash amount. The transaction is complete in seconds.

Core Strategic Value Pillars

This initiative is more than a feature; it's a strategic pivot that delivers compounding value across security, customer experience, and future growth.

Enhanced Security

By removing the physical card from the transaction, we virtually eliminate the risk of skimming. Dynamic OTPs render stolen credentials useless, creating the most secure cash access method on the market.

Unmatched Convenience

Pre-staging transactions reduces time at the ATM from minutes to seconds. This "grab-and-go" experience drastically improves customer satisfaction and reduces queues at high-traffic locations.

Hygienic Advantage

The touchless nature of this solution is a powerful differentiator that directly addresses modern consumer health concerns, making our network the preferred choice for safety-conscious users.

Future-Ready Platform

This architecture establishes a scalable ATM-as-a-Service model. It opens the door for high-margin value-added services like P2P cash transfers, bill payments, and targeted in-app promotions.

Phased Implementation Roadmap

Our proposed timeline ensures a measured, data-driven rollout that mitigates risk while accelerating time-to-value. Each phase includes clear objectives and success metrics.

Phase 1 (Q4 2025 - Q1 2026)

Pilot Program

Deploy to a limited set of 500 ATMs in key urban markets with a partner bank. Validate technology and gather user feedback.

Phase 2 (Q2 2026 - Q4 2026)

Regional Expansion

Roll out to 20,000 ATMs across major regions based on pilot data. Launch marketing campaigns to drive adoption.

Phase 3 (2027)

Full Network Rollout

Enable touchless capabilities across the entire NCR Atleos network. Integrate with multiple banking partners.

Phase 4 (2028+)

Value-Added Services

Launch new revenue-generating features, suchs as 'Send Cash to Others', international remittances, and integrated bill pay.
